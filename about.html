I am from one of the poorest regions in my country with few opportunities to succeed in life, but in that region we value principles like hard word and giving back to the community. I worked as a python developer for some companies before getting into consulting for Deloitte in Senegal. I was the valedictorian in my training at Sonatel Academy.

My decision to apply to MEST was driven by my passion for entrepreneurship and my desire to learn from experts in the software technology development industry. After researching various programs, I was impressed by MEST's focus on providing a comprehensive curriculum that combines technical and business skills. The opportunity to network with successful entrepreneurs and gain hands-on experience through the incubator program was a key factor in my decision to apply.
